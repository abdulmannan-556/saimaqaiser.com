KYC & CDD Policy

Our commitment to compliance, transparency, and responsible business practices.

Purpose

The Know Your Customer (KYC) and Customer Due Diligence (CDD) policy is designed to prevent misuse of financial services, ensure regulatory compliance, and protect both the company and its clients.

Scope

This policy applies to all clients, investors, and business relationships of Saima Qaiser Securities (Pvt) Ltd, in accordance with applicable laws and regulatory guidelines.

Customer Identification

Clients may be required to provide valid identification documents, proof of address, and other relevant information to establish identity.

Due Diligence Measures

  • ✔ Verification of identity
  • ✔ Risk profiling based on customer category
  • ✔ Ongoing monitoring where applicable
  • ✔ Enhanced due diligence for higher-risk cases

Record Keeping

Records related to KYC and CDD are maintained securely for the period required by law and are accessible only to authorized personnel.

Confidentiality

Client information is treated as confidential and is used solely for compliance, regulatory, and operational purposes.

Updates & Amendments

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in regulations or internal compliance requirements.
