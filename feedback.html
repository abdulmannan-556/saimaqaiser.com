Corporate Governance

Our governance framework ensures transparency, accountability, and regulatory compliance.

Governance Philosophy

Saima Qaiser Securities (Pvt) Ltd operates under a governance framework that prioritizes ethical conduct, regulatory compliance, and the protection of stakeholder interests.

Board Oversight

The company is overseen by its management in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, and best practices relevant to the securities industry in Pakistan.

Risk Management

Risk management policies are implemented to identify, assess, and mitigate operational, financial, and compliance risks. Internal controls are reviewed periodically.

Compliance & Ethics

The company adheres strictly to applicable regulatory requirements, including those issued by relevant authorities. Employees are expected to follow a strict code of conduct and ethical standards.

Client Protection

Client confidentiality, fair dealing, and transparent communication form the cornerstone of our relationship with investors. Complaints and feedback are handled through formal procedures.

Continuous Improvement

Governance policies and procedures are reviewed and updated periodically to reflect regulatory changes and evolving industry standards.
